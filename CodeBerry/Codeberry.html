True facts About The Mantis Shrimp

Mantis shrimps are predators which kill their prey by hitting them at high speed. They have large, complex eyes and are highly colored. (As tamarutacas são predadoras que matam as suas presas com ataques em alta velocidade. Elas são bem coloridas e têm olhos grandes e complexos.)

Attack

Mantis shrimps have two methods of attack; they can be spearers or smashers (As tamarutacas têm dois métodos de ataque: elas podem perfurar ou esmagar.) Eyesight

Their eyes are mounted on mobile stalks and constantly move about independently of each other. They are considered to be the most complex eyes in the animal kingdom. (Seus olhos estão montados em hastes móveis e se movem de maneira constante e independente um do outro. Eles são considerados os olhos mais complexos do reino animal.) The mantis shrimp has such good eyes that it can perceive both polarized light and color vision outside the normal visual spectrum. (A tamarutaca tem olhos tão bons que ela pode perceber tanto a luz polarizada quanto cores fora do espectro visual normal.)
